### [Brainstorm Guide](./sc-brainstorm-guide.md)
**Command**: `/sc:brainstorm`

Interactive requirements discovery system that transforms ambiguous ideas into concrete specifications through Socratic dialogue, multi-persona coordination, and systematic exploration.

**Features**:
- 5-phase workflow (Explore ‚Üí Analyze ‚Üí Validate ‚Üí Specify ‚Üí Handoff)
- Multi-persona coordination (architect, analyst, frontend, backend, security, product)
- MCP integration (Sequential, Context7, Magic, Serena)
- Cross-session persistence and iterative refinement

**Best For**:
- New product discovery
- Feature requirements exploration
- Technical architecture decisions
- Cross-session requirement refinement

### [Business Panel Guide](./business-panel-guide.md)
**Command**: `/sc:business-panel`

Multi-expert business analysis system that simulates panel discussions between renowned business thought leaders (Porter, Drucker, Christensen, Taleb, etc.) to analyze documents through multiple strategic frameworks.

**Features**:
- 9 business experts with distinct frameworks
- 3 analysis modes (Discussion, Debate, Socratic)
- Adaptive expert selection
- Cross-framework synthesis

**Best For**:
- Strategic document review
- Risk analysis
- Innovation strategy
- Competitive intelligence
